How to initiate a return
INITIATE YOUR RETURN
ENJOY AN EASY RETURN AND REFUND ONLINE POLICY
You can return all eligible item(s) within 15 days for Official Store and 7 days for Marketplace. Click here to see Frequently asked Questions.
PREPARE THE ITEM
Place the item in its original packaging, including any accessories, tags, labels or freebies. If you have created a password on the device you wish to return, please ensure it is removed, otherwise, your return will be invalid.
DROP OFF THE ITEMS OR SCHEDULE A PICK UP
Our Jumia agent will pick up your item within one business day from the address provided below. Drop off your item at your preferred pickup stations at your earliest convenience to expedite the return processing. Failure to drop off the item within 8 business days from the date you initiated the return will result in the cancellation of your return request.
REFUND PROCESSED
Once we receive your returned item, we will inspect it and process your refund within 24 hours into your preferred refund method.
